WHACK-A-SOMETHING


A Game of Good Versus Evil!

The year is 2018, and America needs you now more than ever! Only you have the power to either lead the American people to a utopian dream, or send it spiraling to a dystopian inferno.
In this game, your clicking skills will be put to the test! Your role is to choose the best hero for the job, and click the enemy to extinction. You will only have 60 seconds, and the enemy will not relent. Power ups will appear to either carry you to victory, or hinder your patriotic cause; so click with caution.

Remember: the future of the United States is literally in your hand!
